Music

Composing melodies that touch the soul and bridge classical tradition with modern innovation

Musical Journey

My musical journey began at age five with classical piano training, evolving through years of formal education and diverse performance experiences. I hold a Master's degree in Music Composition from Juilliard, where I developed my unique voice blending classical foundations with contemporary elements.

My compositions have been performed by symphony orchestras, chamber ensembles, and solo artists across the country. I specialize in creating emotionally resonant pieces that speak to both trained musicians and general audiences.

Visual Storytelling

Photography, for me, is about more than capturing images—it's about preserving emotions, telling stories, and revealing the extraordinary within the ordinary. My work spans portrait photography, fine art, and lifestyle imagery, each project approached with artistic vision and technical precision.

I believe in the power of natural light and authentic moments. Whether working in my studio or on location, I strive to create images that resonate with viewers long after they've seen them. My style blends classical composition with contemporary sensibilities.
