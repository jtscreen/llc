Joshua Screen

Joshua Screen is a New York-based performer with nearly a decade of experience across film, theater, and a variety of performance arts, including music and dance.

With a strong background in both comedic and dramatic roles, he has become known for his dynamic range and versatility on stage and screen. From high-energy performances to nuanced portrayals, he is committed to creating authentic, memorable characters.

Having studied at Fordham University under the guidance of esteemed industry professionals, including Destiny Lily, Dawn Akemi Saito, Andrea Haring, and many others, Joshua has developed a strong foundation in the art of performance. Through their training, he has come to value the significance of collaboration, the discipline of technique, and, above all, the joy and passion that comes with pursuing the craft.

jtscreen

Joshua Screen (jtscreen) is a New York-based music producer, singer/songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist dedicated to the art of music-making.

He began his musical journey at at a young age, studying piano and music theory, and has since expanded his instrumental range to include guitar, bass, drums, alto and baritone saxophone, flute, and ukulele (although he is always looking to add more). This diverse musical background informs both his songwriting and production style, giving him the ability to approach projects with a well-rounded and deeply musical ear.

Jtscreen has taken music production courses during his time studying at his university and primarily uses Logic Pro and Ableton Live. His recording setup includes a Lauten Audio LS-208 microphone and an Apollo Solo audio interface, ensuring industry-standard audio quality.

Whether producing original tracks or collaborating with other artists, jtscreen brings intention, creativity, and technical precision to every project. For collaborations, commissions, or custom production inquiries, please reach out via the contact form or email.

Visual Storytelling

Joshua Screen is a New York City-based photographer with a unique perspective shaped by his experience on both sides of the camera, capturing authentic and meaningful moments, whether it’s a headshot, performance, or special event.

His process is straightforward and client-focused: Before your session, he’ll discuss your vision and reference photos (if applicable). Post-shoot, you’ll receive all images within 1-3 business days. From there, you can select your favorites for editing, with final retouched images delivered promptly.

If you have a custom request or an event outside his listed services, feel free to reach out via his contact form or email to discuss your needs. Joshua prides himself on delivering high-quality work that resonates with every client.
